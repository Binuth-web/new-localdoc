Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Search for a nearby medical center on the homepage, click "View Doctors", and follow the prompts to book an appointment.

Do I need an account to book?

Yes. While you can view available centers and doctors publicly, you must create a Patient account to confirm a booking.

How does the location feature work?

We request your device's GPS coordinates and use the Haversine formula to sort medical centers by physical distance to you.
